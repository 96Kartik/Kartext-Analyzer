• Tokenization

    Tokenization separates text into its most fundamental elements: words. While tokenization is necessary as the basis for text analytics in any language, it is especially important for scripts that do not use spaces between words, like Japanese and Chinese.

  • Sentiment Analysis

    Under this, text is analyzed and then scored for positive and nagative scores of keywords which are further normalized to get the inference or sentiment of the given text.

  • POS Tagging

    Use of machine learning and statistical analysis in Parts of Speech identification to discern the context of a punctuation mark and accurately extract only those which represent sentence boundaries.

  • NER Tagging

    To uncover entities such as people, organizations, and locations and chategorizing them appropriately.

  • Co-referencing

    Returns words or phrases that are semantically similar to the input word or phrase, based on the similar contexts that they appear in.

  • Concept Extraction

    Applying machine learning and deep text mining rules to extract the main concept from the bundles of coherent texts.
